JUDGE QUESTIONS & ANSWERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Q: How do I know the data is real?
A: Visit https://api.fda.gov/ directly - it's the official FDA API. You can
   query it yourself and get the exact same results.

Q: What if your backend is using fake data?
A: The data comes DIRECTLY from FDA. Our backend makes a HTTP request to
   https://api.fda.gov/drug/event.json - it's all public.

Q: Can you verify a specific report?
A: Yes! Every report has a SafetyReportID. Go to FDA.gov and search for that
   ID in their database.

Q: How often is the data updated?
A: FDA FAERS updates weekly. We query live, so users always get current data.

Q: What's the hackathon value of this?
A: Most apps use OLD data or FAKE data. We use LIVE FDA data in real-time.
   This is clinically relevant, not just a demo.
